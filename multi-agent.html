🤖 SkillAI Multi-Agent System

Welcome to SkillAI! A full-scale AI-powered academic learning and career development platform designed for engineering students from Undergraduate through Doctoral levels.

📊 Our 7 Specialized Agents

  • 🧠 Coordinator Agent: Routes your requests to the right expert
  • 📚 Tutor Agent: Explains concepts step-by-step with examples
  • ✏️ Practice Agent: Generates MCQs, problems, and feedback
  • 📖 Exam Agent: Prepares you for university exams
  • 🎯 Career Agent: Guides career paths and skill development
  • 💻 Coding Agent: Teaches programming (Python, Java, C++)
  • 🔬 Research Agent: Supports postgrad and PhD research

🎓 Coverage

Branches: CSE, ECE, ME, EEE, CE, AI&DS, IT, BME

Levels: Undergraduate (Sem 1-8) → M.Tech/MS → PhD

Topics: All university subjects, coding, exams, research

🚀 How to Use

  1. Fill in your profile (branch, semester, career goal)
  2. Enter your query or learning goal
  3. Click "Analyze & Generate Response"
  4. The Coordinator Agent will route to appropriate agents
  5. Explore multiple agent responses using tabs
